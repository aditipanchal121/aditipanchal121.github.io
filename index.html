Hi! My name is Aditi. I am a rising junior Computer Science student at Texas A&M University, in the Engineering Honors Program. Currently, I am interning with Capital One as a Software Engineer. I am proficient in several programming languages and frameworks and am always working on side projects to gain more experience and knowledge. I am excited about data science, and hope to gain more experience with AI by interning in the field and through my honors research thesis.

During quarantine however, I have been traveling vicarously through books and movies.
